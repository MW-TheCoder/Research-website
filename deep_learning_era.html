Deep Learning Era

In the 2000s and 2010s, AI started to grow quickly thanks to deep learning, a type of machine learning that allows computers to learn from massive amounts of data. This led to big improvements in things like voice assistants, facial recognition, and self-driving cars. AI became much better at understanding pictures, text, and even human language. Deep learning made AI more powerful and useful in everyday life.

The 2010s saw a rise in deep learning, allowing AI to process data like never before.

Deep learning revolutionized self-driving cars, natural language processing, and more.
